Operation Manual

HiSight FlightCloud™ is a workflow tool to allow firms to solve changing problems. The platforms are entirely modular and backwards compatible. This means the clients can simply change components depending on requirements.

Most UAVs have fixed flight characteristics which means the customer has to buy a different drone each task. The HiSight™ modular approach allows clients to configure or reconfigure the platforms as required. Drones rarely offer any deep data management capability.

This system allows the client to choose the appropriate data collection instruments and sensors as needed. They can also choose the flight characteristics according to circumstances.

HiSight™ fuses the data with the latest off the shelf software and analytics as needed.
