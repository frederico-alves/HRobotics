Introducing HiSight™ Professional Aerial Platforms

The ability to visualize complex three-dimensional physical environment data is essential in the modern world. The problem with many applications is that the historical data used is static and frequently out of date. But what if the environment could be seen in real-time? This is what HiSight™ delivers.

Technically, HiSight™ uses photogrammetry to create ortho-mosaics in three dimensions enabling real-time collaborative management on smart devices from anywhere in the world. It allows professionals to see, measure, track and act in three dimensions using real-time, proximate, aerial data.

The HiSight™ system comprises a robust physical platform in the sky with a digital platform, FlightCloud™. These meta platforms deploy a wide array of customised and cutting edge multi-role accessories from cameras and sensors to software analytics. It is a rugged, modular, versatile aerial tool that will accomplish many tasks across several industries.

HiSight™ Configurations

Sniffer

Energy firms can find a gas leak on a rig and confer via smart devices in real-time in an emergency but also compare data gathered over time to detect slow moving problems.

Assessor

Insurers can see and assess losses faster and more precisely in three dimensions by combining the real-time information with the data gathered over time.

Reporter

News agencies and broadcasters can deliver video data from multiple cameras faster than internal satellite links. They can safely fly over evenbts relying on in-built parachutes.

Monitor

Police can track and illuminate suspects without being detected. Each team member to see what's happening on each of their hand-held smart devices.

Surveyor

Construction firms can continually survey progress at a site. This allows them to place their materials more precisely which can cut costs. Safety inspections can happen faster and more frequently.

Farmer

Agribusinesses can see where to spray or manage water and fertilizer but also where crops are ripening early, late or need more resources.

Guardian

Mining firms can guard their assets from theft, value their mines and assets the volume of their tailings dams.

Protector

Border authorities can protect physical structures and groups using high definition camerass and facial recognition software.

Auditor

Professionals will soon be able to use Virtual and Mixed Reality which will allow the validation and audit of physically remote assets.
