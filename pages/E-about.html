H Robotics was founded by two professionals who have more than 50 years of experience in working for and with global commercial firms.

They decided to deliver on the Google Map promise by providing HiSight - an aerial meta-data platform system allows companies to see, measure, track and act in 3rd dimension.

It is reliable in wind, rain, water, heat, cold and most weather conditions.

We provide safety accessories like parachutes. HiSight gives clients reliable, real time, proximate, mixed reality (heli)data from photogrammetry to create orthomosaics to 3D models to real-time collaborative crisis management.

The system gives customers sight of their assets.

All live and logged data is accessible on smart devices. We only sell to commercial firms. We serve clients are across many sectors.
